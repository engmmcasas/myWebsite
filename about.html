Hello there!, I´m Manuel M. Casas

I´m an engineer with great passion for implementing data mining, statistical analysis and machine learning techniques to prepare, visualize and create models to resolve complex problems involving data. Right now I´m an AI Engineer at TenarisTamsa helping solve and improve inside processes along with other professionals of different disciplines.

I began this journey when I studied Bioengineering where I focused in bioinstrumentation and digital signal processing to extract valuable features from biomedical data. Then, in my first master´s, working with electrocardiogram signals, I proposed an adaptive infrastructure to separate ventrical vs supraventricular heartbeats using unsupervised machine learning.

Right after my master´s, I entered the industry analyzing retail data and started Spark for Big Data processing. Besides doing data science, I coded ML routines for the inhouse software in Python-PySpark. I moved on to TenarisTamsa where I enriched my skills in all areas of ML Engineer/Data Scientist such as; visualization, ML deployment, statistical data analysis, image and natural language processing, etc.

I am also enrolled in OMSCS GaTech since Fall 2019 majoring in in ML taking mixed classes such as Big Data for Health, Computer Vision, Computational Photography, AI for Robotics, High Dimensional Data Analytics and Computer Networks.

Last but not least, I recently joined AI MEXICO as a Delegate of Veracruz in order to raise awareness of the important opportunity AI represents, connect people and organizations to opportunities between Veracruz and the AI MEXICO Community.
